Kabir Singh is a popular but controversial 2019 Bollywood film about a brilliant but short-tempered surgeon (Shahid Kapoor) who spirals into alcoholism, drugs, and rage after his intense love, Preeti (Kiara Advani), is forced to marry someone else, showcasing a dark, self-destructive path driven by obsessive love, heartbreak, and toxic masculinity, sparking debates on love, gender roles, and redemption, according to reviews from GIPS Hospital, IMDb, and Hindustan Times
